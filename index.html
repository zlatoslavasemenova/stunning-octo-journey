Wireless Charging Dock for Apple Airpods

See All Reviews

  • FAQ

  • Clever and elegant — Designed to rest and charge by your bedside or at your work desk with ease; the Wireless Charging Dock will keep your AirPods safely stored in a familiar location, always within arm’s reach.

    Crafted in beautifully finished anodised Aluminum, it is both a bespoke design statement, subtle enough to complement any environment even when not in use, and your guarantee for fully charged Airpods, always.

    The Charging Dock features an anti-slip rubber base, preventing your AirPods from tumbling to the floor, and ships with a flat tangle free USB-C to USB-A cable in the box.

    Compatibility:
    Works with the latest AirPods with Wireless Charging Case (Gen 2). 

    Pre-Order Today:
    The Wireless Charging Dock is currently available for pre-order at a limited discounted price while we move into production. We expect shipping to commence late 2019, and will update all pre-orders via email as we progress.

  • Does this include the charging cable?
    Yes. A USB-C to USB-A cable is included in the box. Or, if you’d prefer to BYO cable, the Wireless Charging Dock will work with any USB-C cable of your choosing.

    Can I charge other things with this product?
    Yes! You can charge any Qi compatible device with this wireless charger, in fact, it makes an awesome iPhone charger too. But we really just designed it for AirPods.
